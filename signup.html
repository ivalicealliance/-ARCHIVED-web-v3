Please review this agreement and follow these instructions. A free Discord account is required to complete the process.

Select a Final Fantasy Character

You must choose a Final Fantasy Character as your persona. Any characters listed on the Final Fantasy Record Keeper wiki are acceptable. Characters not on this list are also acceptable when verified to be in any Final Fantasy game including spin-offs.

Previously used characters can be re-used if the past member has been inactive for at least 6 months. When re-using, the past member using the name will be versioned by adding a numerical digit, and will select a new character upon reactivation. The new member (you) will not have a digit in your name.

Start a dialogue with a Leader or Judge for any clarification or help on completing this process.

Join the Discord Server

Use this invite link to join the Discord server. Your nickname will be set to the Final Fantasy Character agreed upon. When this process is completed, you will be granted full access to the server.
