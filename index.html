The Ivalice Alliance is a Final Fantasy themed clan that features a unique character system inspired by the Final Fantasy series. The clan operates on a Discord server as a community that supports any topics and games that members are interested in.

Information

  • Founded: February 2nd, 2008
  • Launched: February 17th, 2008

Mission

To facilitate a Final Fantasy themed gaming community with honor, integrity, and excellence.

Leadership

Leaders

Responsible for all daily management of the clan and for implementing short and long term plans. They shall make fair decisions in the best interest of the clan.
