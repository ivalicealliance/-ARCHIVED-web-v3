The Ivalice Alliance is a Final Fantasy themed community that features a unique character system inspired by the Final Fantasy series. It operates on a Discord server as a community that supports any topics and games that members are interested in.

Mission

To facilitate a Final Fantasy themed community with honor, integrity, and excellence.

Leadership

Leaders

Responsible for all daily management of the community and for implementing short and long term plans. They shall make fair decisions in the best interest of the community.
