Welcome to the Ivalice Alliance community! We are a group of fans who are passionate about discussing all things interesting and unique, from video games and anime to music and bar culture. Our community is inspired by the iconic Final Fantasy franchise, and as such, we have a character system where members can pick a name from the series as their persona. This adds an extra layer of fun and immersion to our conversations, which extend beyond just Final Fantasy. We enjoy talking about a wide range of other topics as well.

Whether you're a fan of the series or just looking for a place to chat about your interests with like-minded people, you'll find a welcoming and inclusive community here at the Ivalice Alliance. We embrace diversity and respect all members, regardless of their background or level of knowledge. So come on in, pick your character, and join the conversation! We can't wait to get to know you and discuss all of our shared interests.

Mission

Our mission is to foster a positive and engaging environment for Final Fantasy enthusiasts, where members can share their love for the franchise and its related media with honor, integrity, and excellence.
