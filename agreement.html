Clan Agreement

By joining this clan, you are agreeing to the following rules. Failure to abide by these rules may result in a warning, suspension of clan privileges, or removal from the clan.

  • Respect
    This is the most important rule in the clan. Do not disrespect other members in the clan. If you have an issue with someone, discuss it with them. If you cannot resolve this issue with them, then speak to a leader about it, and they will try to resolve it. Childish name calling and false accusations have no place in this clan.
  • Final Fantasy Character
    Your display name must be a Final Fantasy Character. This is only required on our Discord server and the Forums. It is preferred that you use this character name on other platforms, but not required. You are free to use whatever name you like on any other platforms.
  • Spam
    Do not spam in the clan. No spamming in the channel, no spamming on the forums, and etc.
